About

I design and automate bioinformatics workflows for cancer genomics, transcriptomics, and proteomics research. My work spans somatic variant calling, CNV analysis, mutational signature profiling, and RNA-seq, executed on AWS Batch and HPC using Nextflow. I collaborate with clinicians, wet lab scientists, and researchers to translate large-scale multi-omics datasets into findings that drive precision oncology decisions.

With expertise in Nextflow, AWS Batch, RNA-seq analysis, variant annotation, mutational signature profiling, and copy number variation (CNV) detection, I build scalable, reproducible pipelines for high-throughput sequencing data. My work has contributed to studies on patient-derived xenografts, plasma proteome comparisons, and single-cell transcriptomics, uncovering actionable insights into tumor biology and disease progression.

I hold a Master of Science in Bioinformatics from the University of Michigan, where I conducted research in cancer transcriptomics, developing RNA-seq pipelines to analyze gene expression and uncover regulatory pathways in disease models.

Passionate about data-driven discovery, I aim to bridge biology and computation through robust, automated solutions that translate complex sequencing data into meaningful biological insight.

Fusion Annotator

A deployed web app for classifying EML4-ALK gene fusions from chromosomal breakpoint coordinates. Supports hg19 and hg38, handles single-entry and batch CSV input, and applies canonical variant classification rules including intron-junction detection. Built to remove a recurring manual step from clinical genomics workflows.

Neoantigen Target Discovery

An end-to-end pipeline for identifying neoantigen candidates from somatic mutation data. Processes 1,091 Multiple Myeloma patient MAFs from the MMRF CoMMpass study, extracts missense mutations, constructs 9-mer peptides, and runs MHC-I binding prediction with netMHCpan 4.2c. Identifies recurrent driver mutations in NRAS, KRAS, and BRAF as priority vaccine candidates.

Python netMHCpan 4.2c pandas GDC/MMRF CoMMpass
